  If Dinkar Ltd. has 200 shares forfeited due to non-payment of the final call, what is the total amount of share capital that is subscribed but not fully paid-up?

    A) Rs. 30,000
    B) Rs. 20,000
    C) Rs. 10,000
    D) Rs. 30000 - 200*20
  ✗ Incorrect.
  Correct answer: D) Rs. 30000 - 200*20
  Why: The total amount of share capital that is subscribed but not fully paid-up is the amount received for 300 shares that are not fully paid minus the amount not received for the 200 forfeited shares.
